INAUGARATION CEREMONY

INAUGARATION
The inauguration of the Encrypted Sec Club was a momentous occasion, marking the beginning of an exciting new chapter for our club. The event was graced by the presence of distinguished guests whose words and presence added immense value to the celebration.

The honored guests included our Chief Guest, Tanmay S. Dikshit, Principal A. R. Mirikar, HOD Dr. G. N. Jorvekar, Academic Dean K. P. Jadhav, and Faculty Mr. M. R. Shaikh. Their presence and support were deeply appreciated by everyone involved.

The inauguration ceremony was a great success, filled with inspiring speeches, insightful discussions, and a shared sense of excitement for the future of the Encrypted Sec Club. We look forward to the many achievements and milestones that lie ahead.
