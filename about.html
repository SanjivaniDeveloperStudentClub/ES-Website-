About

Founded in 2023, Encrypted Sec is a dedicated cybersecurity club committed to fostering a secure digital environment within our college community and beyond. Our organization comprises two specialized teams: the Core Team and the Management Team, totaling 11 dedicated members who are passionate about cybersecurity and ethical hacking.

vision

Our vision is to become a leading cybersecurity and ethical hacking club. We are dedicated to fostering a secure digital environment within our college community and envision a future where every student possesses the knowledge and skills to protect themselves online.

mission

​
  • Provide Security:​ Enhance the digital security of our college community by offering guidance and resources to protect personal and academic digital assets.
  • Spread Social Awareness: Raise awareness about cybersecurity risks and best practices among college students and the local community through informative events and campaigns.
